Why Game Design?

When I was looking for what I want to study and what I want to be doing in my live there were just so many different things I all wanted to do, best all at the same time. But I decided for Game Design, because I nearly see all of my different interests concerted within this one studies.

There is the technical side, working with the engine, different systems and programming, there is so much creativity in the world building, character creation, thinking of mechanics, doing level and game design. Then of course there also is the art side, drawing, modeling and animating for which you also need to know biomechanics and right proportions.
